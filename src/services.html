Who We Do

PRE-CONSTRUCTION ANTI TERMITE TREATMENT

This treatment takes place before and during construction. The soil on which the structure is to be erected, the building pillars and other joints are thoroughly treated to prevent termites’ activities afterwards. Pre-construction anti-termite treatment is the most effective way to prevent termite invasion in buildings or homes. Treating the soil on which your structure sits creates chemical barrier between the ground and brickwork of the foundation to avoid termites’ access to any part of the building. Termites popularly known as white ants cause considerable damage to wood works, furnishing etc. We are the best in terminating and preventing termite infestation and will provide you with ten (10) years written guarantee. Call us today for a FREE consultation.
